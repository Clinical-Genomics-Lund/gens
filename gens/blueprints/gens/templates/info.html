{% macro help_popup() %}

Basic navigation

  • Use left and right button to navigate the chromosome
  • Use zoom buttons to zoom in and out the chromosome

Mouse navigation

  • Navigate to a chromosome by click it the overview figure
  • click and dragging with the mouse to move the region
  • Zoom in coverage figure by Shift+click
  • Zoom in on a region by holding Shift and dragging with the mouse
  • Zoom out in coverage figure by Ctrl+click

Keybord shortcuts

  • Use left and right arrow keys to change chromosome
  • Goto a chromosome by press chromosome number + Enter

Search

To search type your query into the search box. Available queries are:

  • chromosome:start-stop
  • chromosome
  • gene name
